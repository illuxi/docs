## Explanations of some fields

| Name | Explanation |
| --- | --- |
| Generate a certificate | If enabled, a certificate for the bundle will be available to users<br /> who complete each product in the bundle. |
| Contact person | If a user is selected, the question or comment form on the right<br />side of the bundle’s catalog page will be sent to that user. <br />If no user is selected, the form will be sent to the contact email <br />configured in the Email tab of the Settings page. |
| Added to home spotlight? | **If enabled and an image spotlight has been uploaded,** <br />**the image will be added to the homepage slideshow.** |
